# Lavender Health Tracker

A gentle companion for your personal wellness journey. Track, reflect, and bloom at your own rhythm.

## Motivation and Disclaimer

My motivation is driven by my wife. She requested a health tracker application that is end-to-end encrypted, and I consider it to be a wonderful opportunity to collaborate on something she needed.

I am a male developer, and I am approaching this with humility. I do not claim to understand a woman's experience at all. I do understand how a beautiful, data-driven, and secure software can add value to a woman's life.

Feedback from women is very welcome!

## Privacy & Security

- End-to-end encryption (keys never leave your device)
- Zero third-party data sharing
- Local-first architecture
- Open source for full transparency
